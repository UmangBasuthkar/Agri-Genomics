Home


Welcome to our project on "Genomic Prediction of Oryza Sativa Plant Height using Machine Learning." We aim to revolutionize agricultural genomics by using machine learning algorithms to predict rice plant height. By analyzing genomic data and leveraging advanced techniques, we can provide valuable insights for optimizing crop management and enhancing yield. Join us as we explore the potential of machine learning in improving rice production and food security.

About Us


We are a dedicated team of researchers leveraging cutting-edge genomics and machine learning technologies to enhance agricultural practices. Our project focuses on predicting the plant height of Oryza Sativa (rice) through genomic analysis and advanced predictive models. With a mission to drive precision agriculture and improve crop productivity, we collaborate with stakeholders and strive to make a tangible impact on global food security. Join us as we revolutionize the field of agricultural genomics through innovation and collaboration.
